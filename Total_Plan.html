Weigh everything. Log oils and condiments. Unweighed portions add 200–400 kcal/day — enough to erase your deficit.

Recalculate calories at every 7 kg lost. Body at 84 kg burns ~150 kcal/day less than at 104.3 kg. Recalculate or plateau.

😴 Sleep Rule

7.5–9 hrs non-negotiable. Under 6.5 hrs: hunger hormones spike 30%, muscle synthesis drops 18%. In bed by 9:30 PM.

🍠 Option B: Sweet Potato

Replace buckwheat: 130g baked flesh (lunch) + 100g (dinner). Saves ~15–20 kcal/day. More Vitamin A + potassium. Everything else identical.

📈 Progressive Overload

Every 1–2 wks: add a rep, increase weight, add a set, or cut rest by 10 sec. Track every weight every session. You cannot overload what you don't measure.

🔄 Refeed Days (Month 6+)

Every 10–14 days: ~3,000 kcal, high carb (buckwheat, sweet potato, oats), protein 195g+. Restores leptin. This is a tool, not a cheat day.
